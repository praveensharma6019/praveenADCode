#AmdavadProgressiveChhe

It encapsulates various aspects that make Ahmedabad a truly progressive city and touches upon relevant points like what progress means to different people. Through interactive sessions on the Radio, it highlights some true stories from the city, which bring to fore the progressive side of the city, but in a warm and heartfelt manner. The story of a father supporting and even encouraging his daughter's move to a different corner of the city to be close to her office!
